"White Sauce Pasta"

"White sauce pasta is a creamy and delicious dish made with pasta, a white sauce (b√©chamel), and cheese. It's a comforting meal that can be customized with vegetables or proteins."

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the flour and stir for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Gradually add the milk, stirring constantly until thickened.
  5. Add cheese, salt, and pepper. Stir until melted.
  6. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce and serve hot.
